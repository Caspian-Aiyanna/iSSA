About RSF Maps

Realized Occupancy: Empirical density of point locations. Shows where the elephant actually spent time.

Habitat Usage: Predicted selection based on environmental covariates (vegetation, water, terrain).

  • Switch between Realized Occupancy and Habitat Usage.
